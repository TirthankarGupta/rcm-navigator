[AETNA MEDICARE]

Aetna is out-of-network for CPO.
Ensure POS is either 22 [Out Patient] or 23 [ER Visit].
Bill to Insurance only if within 48 hours of delivery.
Match patient name.
Check Referring physician info - 'Should be filled and green ticked'.
Modifiers only 'LT|RT & KX'.
For Abdominal Binders use 'CG'.
For CO-prefab use ONLY 'KX'.
For Walker/Wheelchair/Crutches/HO-Prefab 'NU & KX'.
Split as necessary.
Box 24 [NPI] = Box 33a = 1174573307.
Box 31 [Signature of Physician or Supplier] CPO Services Inc.
Box 32 [Service Facility Information] KEEP IT 'BLANK'.
Submit through clearinghouse.
